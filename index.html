Welcome to Our Beach Cleaning Initiative

We are a group of enthusiastic high school students from Taiwan, committed to protecting our coastlines and marine life. Our initiative focuses on organizing beach clean-ups, educating the community, and advocating for environmental conservation.

Why Beach Cleaning Matters

Clean beaches are vital for the health of marine ecosystems and local communities. Beach cleaning helps to remove harmful debris, protect marine wildlife, and preserve the natural beauty of our coastlines. It also raises awareness about the impact of pollution and encourages sustainable practices.

Beach Cleaning Event Details

Join us on July 21, 2023, at the Golden Coast in Tainan. We will meet at 9:00 AM near the main beach entrance. Participants are encouraged to wear comfortable clothing and bring reusable water bottles. All cleaning materials will be provided. Let's work together to make a positive impact!
